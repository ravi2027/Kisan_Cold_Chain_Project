The Problem

Cold storage is built for cities. Farmers live in villages.

Most cold storage capacity sits in metros while perishable harvests rot in rural districts. Transport is informal, weather is unpredictable, and mandi prices are opaque. The result: ₹92,000 crore in spoilage every single year.

  • ● No cold storage within 50 km of most villages
  • ● Informal transport with no temperature control
  • ● No real-time mandi price information
  • ● No way to share trucks with neighboring farmers
